# CLAIM 3: Vested Rights

## Legal Elements
- **A-Prior Existing Use**: Mining/extraction operations predated the ordinance
- **B-Substantial Investment**: Substantial financial investment made in reliance
- **C-Good Faith Reliance**: Good faith reliance on existing legal framework
- **D-Retroactive Destruction**: Government cannot retroactively destroy vested rights
