# CLAIM 1: Ordinance Invalid / State CIM Preemption

## Legal Elements
- **A-Preemption**: State CIM statute (17-41-402) preempts county authority
- **B-County Knowledge**: County knew or should have known ordinance violated state law
- **C-Adoption Despite Warnings**: Ordinance adopted despite legal warnings
- **D-Bad Faith**: County acted in bad faith or with improper motive
- **General-Ordinance**: General ordinance/zoning relevance
