Dear Colleagues:
Consistent with Utah Code Ann. § 17-53-205, Section 2.09 of the Optional Plan for
Salt Lake County Government, and Section 2.04.090 of Salt Lake County Ordinance, I
hereby exercise my prerogative as the Chair of the Salt Lake County Council to order
a special meeting of the Salt Lake County Council to be held on December 10, 2021,
at 09:30 A.M. in Room N1-100 of the Salt Lake County Government Center.

District 2

Aimee Winder Newton
District 3

Ann Granato
District 4

Dea Theodore
District 6

The purpose of the proposed meeting will be for the Salt Lake County Council to
formally initiate proceedings to amend ordinance, pursuant to Utah Code Section
17-27a-508(1)(a)(ii)(B), by submitting proposed amendment to planning commission
for recommendation pursuant to Salt Lake County Code Section 19.90.010. The
proposed ordinance will amend section 19.12.030 of the Salt Lake County Code to
eliminate mineral extraction and processing as a conditional use and explicitly
prohibit the same and similar uses in the Forestry and Recreation zones; amend
section 19.72.190 to eliminate mineral extraction and processing as a use for which
waivers can be granted; amend section 19.76.030 regarding classification of
permitted and conditional uses not listed in title 19; and enact related regulations. A
copy of the proposed agenda for this special meeting is attached to this order.
Council staff shall provide notice of this special meeting to the Salt Lake County
Council and Salt Lake County Mayor, and shall also provide notice to the public and
media as required by the Utah Open and Public Meetings Act, Utah Code Ann. § 524-101 et seq., and Salt Lake County Ordinance. A copy of this order shall be included
in the minutes of the special meeting.
