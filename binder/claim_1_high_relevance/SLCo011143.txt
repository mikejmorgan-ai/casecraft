Public Notice

The Salt Lake County Council hereby provides public notice that it is formally initiating proceedings to
amend ordinance, pursuant to Utah Code Section 17-27a-508(1)(a)(ii)(B), by submitting the proposed
amendment to the planning commission for recommendation pursuant to Salt Lake County Code
Section 19.90.010.
The proposed ordinance is to amend section 19.12.030 of the Salt Lake County Code to eliminate
mineral extraction and processing as a conditional use and explicitly prohibit the same and similar uses
in the Forestry and Recreation zones; amend section 19.72.190 to eliminate mineral extraction and
processing as a use for which waivers can be granted; amend section 19.76.030 regarding classification
of permitted and conditional uses not listed in title 19; and to enact related regulations.
The proposed ordinance and any related matters will be discussed at a special meeting of the Salt Lake
County Council to be held on December 10, 2021, at 9:30 am. A meeting agenda and supporting
documents will be published separately from this notice and in a manner consistent with the Utah Open
and Public Meetings Act.
