We must prioritize the environment and recreation in our canyons, and we are.

our environment so that future generations may not only live healthy lives, but also experience
the same awe and wonder we get to enjoy today every time we’re outdoors.
I want to thank our planning commissions, staff, and Council for their work.
In today’s world it can be a challenge to find consensus.
But the public, my fellow leaders and policy makers in the valley have been resolute.
Public support submitted to the County -- spanning more than 1,000 comments -- was
unanimous.
