Dear Members of the Board of the Division of Oil, Gas, and Mining,
As a resident of Sugar House in Salt Lake City, I am deeply opposed to the proposed mine in Parley's Canyon.
The reasons why I oppose this project include:
1. Protecting public health
2 Protecting critical Wasatch Mountain watershed and water resources
3. Protecting and valorizing Parley’s Canyon flora and fauna
4. Preserving the structural integrity of nearby homes
5. Preserving the aesthetic beauty of Parley’s Canyon
6. Avoiding an increased risk of wildfires in the canyon
7. Protecting the property value of my home. I live approximately two miles away from the proposed mine, and I'm
concerned about its impact on the value of my property, which is a wealth builder for my family.
Thank you for considering my concerns.
Sincerely,
Alessandro Rigolon
Sugar House resident
