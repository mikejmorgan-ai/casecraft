## 🚨 **STRATEGIC FILTER: IGNORE COUNTY DISTRACTIONS**

### **"SAND AND GRAVEL PITS" = RED HERRING**
The County will attempt to distinguish "sand and gravel pits" from "mining" to avoid state preemption. **This is a legal distraction.**

**Utah Statutory Definition of Mining:**
- Utah Code § 40-8-4: Mining includes "extraction of coal, oil, gas, and **minerals**"
- Utah Code § 40-8-2: Minerals include "limestone, **building stone**"
- Federal Patents: Tree Farm holds patents for "Building Stone & Limestone"
- **Legal Conclusion:** Limestone quarrying IS mining under Utah law

**Counter-Strategy:**
"Whether the County calls it 'sand and gravel' or 'limestone quarrying,' Utah Code defines mining to include extraction of building stone and limestone. Tree Farm holds federal patents for 'Building Stone & Limestone' - exactly what Utah law defines as mining subject to state preemption."
