Salt Lake County has blocked new mining, but will the zoning ordinance hold up against legal challenges? (The
Salt Lake Tribune, Brian Maffly)
“It’s a visual impact. It’s a potential water quality impact. It’s a local impact on the residents,” said Mayor Jenny

weather that no amount of mitigation can prevent.
