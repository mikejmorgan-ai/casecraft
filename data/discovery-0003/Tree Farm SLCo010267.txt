MOTION
Tree Farm’s position in this matter continues to be misstated by the Division. The

Division has stated that under Tree Farm’s position, the Division would lack authority to approve
notices of intention. That is not the case. Under Tree Farm’s position, the Division has authority
to approve small mine notices of intention, but does not have authority to deny them. This is
wholly consistent with the language of the Act. The Division is “not required” to approve notices
of intention for small mining operations, but under governing law, the Division must review
them for completeness. A “denial” of a small mine notice of intention is not contemplated
anywhere in the language of the Act or the rules.
The Division has filed multiple responses in this matter. Tree Farm should be allowed to
address the items raised by the Division. For convenience, the Sur-Reply is being filed
concurrently herewith, on the date that was previously agreed to by the Division for Tree Farm to
file a reply to the Division’s response to any amended request for agency action (February 18),
