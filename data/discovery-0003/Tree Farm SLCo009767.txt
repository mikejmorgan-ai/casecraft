FILED
JANUARY 27 2022
SECRETARY, BOARD OF
OIL, GAS & MINING

To Julie Carter
The Sugar House Community Council would like to offer our support to the community
members who are opposing the Parley’s Canyon Mine. We have talked to their group a
handful of times and they are making a formal presentation at our Feb. 2nd Sugar
House Community Council Meeting. Sugar House would be severely impacted by this
proposed Parley’s Canyon Mine. The size and scope of this mine seems
overwhelming. The amount of traffic from the trucks, the increase in dust and air
pollution and the risk to our water supply are some of the reasons we oppose this
proposal.
Sincerely,

Landon Clark
Landon Clark
Chair, Sugar House Community Council
