FEBRUARY AGENDA
BEFORE THE BOARD OF OIL, GAS AND MINING
DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES
IN AND FOR THE STATE OF UTAH
A public meeting will be held on Wednesday, February 23, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. The
meeting will be conducted electronically with an anchor location at the Department of
Natural Resources Auditorium, 1594 W. North Temple, Salt Lake City, Utah,
pursuant to Utah Code Ann. § 52-4-207.
The meeting will be streamed live on the Department of Natural Resources YouTube
channel. The link for the Briefing Session is https://youtu.be/4Ce0JlLJCGo, and the
link for the Board Hearing is https://youtu.be/k710lcaufbE.
The Division of Oil, Gas and Mining will conduct a Briefing Session at 9:00 a.m.:
1.

Legislative Update

2.

3.

4.

Next Month’s Agenda and Division Calendar

5.

Board Training Working Lunch
A training session for Oil, Gas and Mining Board members may be held, as time
allows, during a virtual working lunch. The working lunch session is open to
the public and will be streamed live on the Department of Natural Resources
YouTube channel at the link shown above for the Board hearing, which is
https://youtu.be/k710lcaufbE.

6.

Opportunity for Public Comment
Members of the public who wish to participate in the meeting electronically or
in-person must contact the Board’s secretary, Ms. Carter, by phone at 801-5385277, or email at juliecarter@utah.gov, at least 24 hours prior to the meeting.
Attendees will be required to abide by all public health protocols in place at the
time of the meeting.

Docket No. 2022-003 Cause No. 130-11 – In the matter of the Request for
Agency Action of ALTAMONT ENERGY OPERATING, LLC for an order amending

Docket No. 2022-002 Cause No. S/035/0053 – In the matter of the Request for
