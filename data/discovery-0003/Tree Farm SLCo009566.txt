I am unable to attend the Department of Oil, Gas and Mining Board Meeting on January 26th, but as
a member of the Richards Family Partnership, I wish to express my opposition to any new mining in
Parleys Canyon which will scar the hillsides and create an ugly blemish in the beautiful terrain of the
canyon.
Specifically, I would urge the members of the Department of Oil, Gas, and Mining Board to vote
against Tree Farm, LLC's current proposed Limestone Quarry in the Mt Aire Canyon area, no matter
how small they suggest they would begin. Once a project gets approved, who knows how wide and
how large it would eventually become.
The curent landowners and cabin owners in the area are very happy with the serene and magnificent
beauty surrounding them in this section of the Mt Aire and Parleys Canyon property. Please, let's
leave it well enough alone!
