This is exactly what we were afraid would happen. Tree Farm is withdrawing both their NOI's and planning to refile only
an SMO NOI. So their appeal has been withdrawn and has been removed from the DOGM board agenda on Wednesday.
The administrative rule governing SMO NOIs is
at https://fs.ogm.utah.gov/PUB/MINES/Minerals_Related/Information/R647-3.pdf
DOGM has 15 days to determine the completeness of an SMO NOI and 10 days to respond to any subsequent filings of
information they request.
There is no provision for a public comment period for an SMO NOI.
