County Council Meeting Agenda December 10, 2021
2.1 Formal initiation of proceedings to amend ordinance, pursuant to Utah Code Section
17-27a-508(1)(a)(ii)(B), by submitting proposed amendment to planning commission for
recommendation pursuant to Salt Lake County Code Section 19.90.010.
Proposed ordinance is to amend section 19.12.030 of the Salt Lake County Code to
eliminate mineral extraction and processing as a conditional use and explicitly prohibit the
same and similar uses in the Forestry and Recreation zones; amend section 19.72.190 to
eliminate mineral extraction and processing as a use for which waivers can be granted;
amend section 19.76.030 regarding classification of permitted and conditional uses not
listed in title 19; and to enact related regulations
