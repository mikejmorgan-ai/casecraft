MARCH AGENDA
BEFORE THE BOARD OF OIL, GAS AND MINING
DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES
IN AND FOR THE STATE OF UTAH
A public meeting will be held on Wednesday, March 23, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. The meeting will
be conducted electronically with an anchor location at the Department of Natural Resources
Auditorium, 1594 W. North Temple, Salt Lake City, Utah, pursuant to Utah Code Ann. § 524-207.
The meeting will be streamed live on the Department of Natural Resources YouTube channel.
The link for the Briefing Session is https://youtu.be/QAPaGxByxxk, and the link for the Board
Hearing is https://youtu.be/oQo_q21E_T4.
The Division of Oil, Gas and Mining will conduct a Briefing Session at 9:00 a.m.:
1.

Legislative Update

2.

Rulemaking

3.

Other

4.

Next Month’s Agenda and Division Calendar

5.

Board Working Lunch
Environmental Excellence Award nominations will be discussed and a training
session for Board members may be held, as time allows. The session is open to the
public and will be streamed live on https://youtu.be/oQo_q21E_T4 (The training
session was continued from the February 23 Board Hearing and may be continued to
the Board’s regularly scheduled hearing on April 27).

6.

Opportunity for General Public Comment
Members of the public who wish to participate in the meeting electronically or inperson must contact the Board’s secretary, Ms. Carter, by phone at 801-538-5277, or
email at juliecarter@utah.gov, at least 24 hours prior to the meeting. Attendees will
be required to abide by all public health protocols in place at the time of the meeting.

The Board will hear the following Matters at or around 10:00 a.m.:
1.
Per Acre Bonding Costs for Small Mines and Exploration – The Division will present
information about average costs per acre for reclamation, followed by an opportunity for
public comment. The Board will be asked to approve a figure that will be used for bonding
small mines and exploration operations.
