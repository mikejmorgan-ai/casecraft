JANUARY AGENDA
BEFORE THE BOARD OF OIL, GAS AND MINING
DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES
IN AND FOR THE STATE OF UTAH
A public meeting will be held on Wednesday, January 26, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. The
meeting will be conducted electronically without an anchor location, pursuant to
Utah Code Ann. § 52-4-207(5)(a)(i).
The meeting will be streamed live on the Department of Natural Resources YouTube
channel. The link for the Briefing Session is https://youtu.be/WV5T6XHzg2I, and the
link for the Board Hearing is https://youtu.be/QmfRXEf2qE4.

Members of the public who wish to participate in the meeting electronically or inperson must contact the Board’s secretary, Ms. Carter, by phone at 801-538-5277, or
email at juliecarter@utah.gov, at least 24 hours prior to the meeting.
