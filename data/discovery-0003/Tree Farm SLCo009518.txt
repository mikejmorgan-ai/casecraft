The Board of Oil, Gas and Mining, having considered the Stipulated Motion to Continue
Hearing and Establish Briefing Schedule (the “Motion”), and finding good cause therefor, hereby
GRANTS the Motion and continues the hearing until February 23, 2022, and authorizes the
proposed briefing schedule set forth in the Motion, which is:
a. January 26, 2022: Deadline for Tree Farm to file its reply to the Response or a
motion to amend the Request together with an amended request for agency
action, and its response to the Motion to Intervene.
b. February 10, 2022: Deadline for the Division to file its response to any
amended request for agency action (if any), and for Millcreek to file any reply
to Tree Farm’s response to the Motion to Intervene.
