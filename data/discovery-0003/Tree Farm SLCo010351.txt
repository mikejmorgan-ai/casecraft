Julie et al,
I would like to voice my strong opposition to the proposed mine in Parleys Canyon. I feel that this
mine poses too many risks for our precious watershed, forest, wildlife, and mountains that are
diminishing, without any clear benefit to our community. It will be an eyesore as other open mines are
to all (N Salt Lake, Rio Tinto) in a canyon that is far more visible and enjoyed now than any other I
know of in Utah. Please stop this mine and preserve our canyons.
