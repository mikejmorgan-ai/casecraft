PROJECT INFORMATION
Final scope of work. Brief description of project including construction methods, equipment, etc. to be employed to complete the work

Tree Farm LLC is proposing to widen and improve an existing dirt road located in Parley's Canyon,
south of Mt. Aire Road, on the east side of I-80 near Mile Post 132. Road improvements would include
widening, stabilizing slopes, and adding gravel. The project also proposes to pipe a portion of Smith
Creek to construct a parking area adjacent to the road.
