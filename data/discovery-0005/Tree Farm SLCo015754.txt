Subject: MPD PC Decision and Update
Greetings,
The Mountainous Planning District Planning Commission unanimously recommended approval to the Salt Lake County
Council that they adopt the proposed ordinance amendment to prohibit mineral extraction and processing in the FR zone.
On February 16th the Salt Lake County Planning Commission will hold a public hearing to consider this same ordinance
and make a recommendation to Council.
The meeting date for a public hearing before the Salt Lake County Council has not been established yet. It is likely that the
public hearing date will be set after the SLC PC holds their public hearing and makes a recommendation to Council.
The MSD received an application for a business license for mining in the FR zone by Tree Farm, LLC, but it was formally
denied by the Salt Lake County Mayor and MSD. The applicant did not appeal that decision within the specified time limit.
To date, the MSD has not received a conditional use permit application from Tree Farm, LLC for mining in Parleys Canyon.
If the MSD does receive a CUP application, it will be on hold while the proposed ordinance amendment is being
considered for adoption. The County has six months from Dec. 10, 2021 to make a decision on the proposed ordinance
amendment before MSD would have to process a CUP application for mining in the FR zone.
Feel free to contact me if you have any questions about this matter.
