Dear Council Members,
I am a lifelong Utahn, a resident of Millcreek, and a local business owner (a law firm in downtown SLC). I write to
communicate my support of the 30-day mask mandate proposed by the health department and to ask that
you support the mask mandate at tomorrow’s council meeting.
A mask mandate will not fix the surge of Omicron—but it could help. Wearing a mask in enclosed public spaces
is a minor and reasonable intervention to help slow the spread of this respiratory virus, reduce strain on our
overburdened healthcare system, and allow businesses to keep operating.
I support the 30-day mask mandate and respectfully ask that you do so, too.
