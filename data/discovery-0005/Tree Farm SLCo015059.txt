Ms. Carter,
I am a member of a family that a own property in Mt. Aire Canyon. I have several concerns with the
Parley Canyon Mining plans. Environmentally, what will the impact be in water use for the mine?
Water is a valuable commodity in the canyon. I also see an increased risk of fire. The impact on air
quality is another concern with the dust created through the mining process. What impact will this
have on the plants and trees as well as air quality in the canyon. I understand that access to the
mine will be through the Mt. Aire gate. I think I read that up to 30 trucks a day will be traveling in and
out of that gate to the mine along with family cars of the cabin owners. This seems like an unsafe
combination of traffic and a risk to families especially in the winter time when the hill up from the gate
can be snowy and icy. I hate the thoughts of a big mining truck losing traction coming down the hill
behind a family car stopping to open the gate. Gate security is also a concern.
The negative impact of this mine in this area can't be emphasized enough. I am very happy that the
Salt Lake County Commision opposes this mineral extraction plan and ask you to please take the
same position.
Sincerely,
Barbara Schmidt
