Hello Julie,

I am hearing from many constituents concerned about the possibility of a new mine in Parley’s Canyon. I
request a moratorium on approval of any more mining permits until OMG can review its approval system to avoid
extractive companies circumventing the intended system of checks and balances set up to protect Salt Lake
County residents and our precious and finite natural resources.

Parley’s Canyon already has a large mine on the north side of the road. For the benefit of our air and water
quality and to preserve the beauty and ecology of this canyon, please ensure this mining operation is not able to
proceed without further review.
