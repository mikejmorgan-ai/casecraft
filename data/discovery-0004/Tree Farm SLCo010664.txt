I am writing in regard to my opposition to the mine that has been proposed in Parley’s Canyon.

It is outrageous to sully the air/water/land with more pollution, vehicle emissions and traffic.

Utah is one of the most beautiful states in the Union, and it is incomprehensible to me that the
natural beauty and resources are being treated with so little respect.

Remember, tourism is a big part of the income of this state and when people see the beauty of
Parley’s Canyon being treated so poorly it will reflect poorly on the state and those who are
charged with its protection and care.
