RE: Recommendation to Deny Business License Application #COM21-1590
Dear Mayor Wilson:
Pursuant to Salt Lake County Code Sections 5.02.080 and 5.02.110, I am submitting the business
license application from Tree Farm, LLC to you for consideration, together with a recommendation
for denial of the business license from MSD Planning and Development Services (“MSD”), the
County’s contracted business licensing official.
Salt Lake County Code Section 19.84.020 provides: “A conditional use permit shall be required for all uses
listed as conditional uses in the district regulations or elsewhere in this title.” The MSD has confirmed that Tree
Farm does not have a conditional use permit for the property that is the subject of this business license
application.
Salt Lake County Code Section 5.02.120 also provides: “A. No license shall be issued for the conduct of any
business, and no permit shall be issued for any activity if the premises and building to be used for the purposes do not
fully comply with the requirements of the county. B. No license or permit shall be issued for the conduct of any business
or performance of any act that would violate building or zoning ordinances of the county.”
Because the property that is the subject of the business license application does not have a conditional
use permit, it is not in compliance with the Salt Lake County zoning ordinance/requirements of the
County. Accordingly, the MSD recommends that you deny Tree Farm’s business license application.

manderson@msd.utah.gov.
