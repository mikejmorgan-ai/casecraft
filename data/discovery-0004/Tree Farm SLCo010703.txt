Dear OGM Board,
As the elected representative of Cottonwood Heights City District 4, we have born the burden of a 150 acre concrete excavation site
bordering on our residential areas for decades. The airborn dust, noise and heavy commercial truck commerce at the mouth of Utah’s
highly esteemed Big Cottonwood Canyon has been a travesty but was approved in a time when this part of Salt Lake Valley was
sparsely populated.
My constituents are opposed to further degrading of the Wasatch Mountain areas located anywhere in or near recreational or
residential areas.
Respectfully, I implore you to place a moratorium on approval of any more mining permits until OMG can sort out its approval
system to avoid extractive companies circumventing the intended system of checks and balances set up to protect SLCO residents and
our precious and finite natural resources.
Let’s have safer mobility, healthier citizens and our best quality of life,

Ellen Birrell
