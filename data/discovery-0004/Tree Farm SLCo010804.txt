THIS SUPPLEMENTAL DECLARATION AND NOTICE OF VESTED MINING USE
("Declaration") is made by the undersigned mine operator (the tei in "mine operator" includes
any natural person, corporation, association, partnership, receiver, trustee, executor,
administrator, guardian, fiduciary, agent, or other organization or representative, either public or
private, including a successor, assign, subsidiary, related parent company, and predecessor in
interest, all of which are collectively referred to herein as "Declarant") of the real property
legally described on Exhibit A attached hereto and incorporated herein by this reference (the
"Property"). Declarant is the successor in interest to P.G.L.C., LLC, R.R.L.C., LLC, Rock &
Roll Land Company, Inc., Pharaohs Glen Land Company, Inc., Parleys Canyon Aggregate
Company, Ira Sachs, Old Spanish Claim, L.C., Old Spanish Claim, L.L.C., Old Spanish Claim,
LLC, who, together with Harper Contracting, Inc., Rulon Harper Construction, Inc. and Rulon J.
Harper executed that certain Declaration of Vested Mining Use recorded as Entry No. 13131633
in the official records of Salt Lake County on November 22, 2019 at Book 10864, Pages 630 to
639. Declarant is also the successor in interest to Shaun Nacey and Marla Nacey, who executed
that certain Supplemental Declaration and Notice of Vested Mining Use recorded as Entry No.
13586235 in the official records of Salt Lake County on March 4, 2021 at Book 11130, Pages
635-637.
Pursuant to Utah Code § 17-41-501(4), the undersigned Declarant hereby provides notice
of a vested mining use on the Property as follows:
A.
This Declaration is intended to be a declaration of a vested mining use on the
Property in accordance with Utah Code §§ 17-41-501 to -503 (2021).
B.
Declarant owns or controls the Property including, without limitation, the surface
and subsurface estates, mineral estates, fee estates, leasehold estates, easement estates, undivided
interests, and other mineral interests, rights, and leases comprising the Property, together with all
rights, privileges, easements, tenements, hereditaments, rights of way, leases, licenses, and
appurtenances that belong or appertain thereto including, without limitation, rights to minerals,
water, water rights and water stock, buildings, structures, and other improvements.
The purpose of this Supplemental Declaration is made pursuant to Paragraph L of that certain Declaration of
Vested Mining Use recorded as Entry No. 13131633 in the official records of Salt Lake County on November 22,
2019 at Book 10864, Pages 630 to 639 to make clear that Tree Farm, LLC now owns or controls the Property
described on Exhibit A and holds a vested mining use pursuant to the 2021 version of the vested mining use statute,
which is codified at Utah Code § 17-41-501 to -503 (2021).

C.
A mining use, as defined in Utah Code § 17-41-101(16), existed or was conducted
or was otherwise engaged in on the Property by Portland Cement Company of Utah, which
changed its name to Utah Portland Quarries, Inc. ("Portland Cement"), and others, at least as
early as the 1890s, before statehood, and well before Salt Lake County restricted or otherwise
limited mining use in the area. Evidence of such prior mining operations is readily apparent from
a physical inspection of the Property and historic records.
D.
Beginning in 1992, and on various dates prior to October 1997, Declarant's
predecessors acquired the Property with the intent to mine it.
E.
Since at least 1993, Declarant's predecessors have operated as a joint venture
regarding the Property.
F.
In 1995, a Notice of Intention to Commence Large Mining Operations with
respect to a portion of the Property (the "Large Mine Permit") was filed with the Utah
Department of Natural Resources' Division of Oil, Gas and Mining ("DOGM") by Rock and
Roll Land Company, Inc., as owner, and Harper Contracting, Inc., as operator. DOGM approved
the Large Mine Permit in 1996 and the Large Mine Permit remains active today.
G.

The Property has been continuously used or held for use for mining operations.

H.
Commercial quantities of a mineral deposit have been produced from the Property
dating back to the earliest mining activity. In 1994, over 90,000 tons of a mineral deposit were
mined from the Property. In 2009, over 725,000 tons of a mineral deposit were mined from the
Property. In 2019, over 386,485 tons of a mineral deposit were mined from the Property.
I.
Declarant qualifies as a "mine operator" under Utah Code § 17-41-101(13)
because, as of January 1, 2019, Declarant (a) owned, controlled, or managed a mining use under
the Large Mine Permit; and (b) had produced and continue to produce commercial quantities of a
mineral deposit from the mining use.
J.
Pursuant to Utah Code § 17-41-101(15), the Property is a part of the mining
protection area and is entitled to all rights, benefits, and protections under Utah Code, Title 17,
Chapter 41.
K.
Pursuant to Utah Code § 17-41-501(3), the present or future boundary described
in the Large Mine Permit does not limit the scope of the Declarant's vested mining rights and
does not limit the protection provided under Utah law for a mining protection area.
L.
Declarant reserves the right, pursuant to Utah Code § 17-41-502 to expand the
vested mining use. Declarant may progress, extend, enlarge, grow, or expand the vested mining
use to any surface or subsurface land or mineral estate that the mine operator owns or controls,
Utah Code § 17-41-502(1)(a); and Declarant may expand the vested mining use to any new land
that (i) is contiguous and related in mineralization to surface or subsurface land or a mineral
estate that the mine operator already owns or controls; (ii) contains minerals that are part of the
