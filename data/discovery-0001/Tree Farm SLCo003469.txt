To whom it may concern:
On January 26, 2022, the Division of Oil, Gas and Mining (Division) received an Amended Notice of Intention
to Commence Large Mining Operations (Notice) for the 1-80 South Quarry (formally Silver Mine by Tree Farms
LLC). This amendment updates contact information to the new operator Granite Construction. Please be aware
that because this plan is not approved, the Division cannot approve this amendment.
There are still several outstanding deficiencies from the last review that need to be address prior to approval.
Therefore, the Division is returning this amendment. With any new responses or amendment please make sure
that this amendment is incorporated into the plan and that Granite Construction signs future submittals including
the mine signature page.
All future correspondence should reference M/035/0054. Thank you for your help in keeping this permit current.
