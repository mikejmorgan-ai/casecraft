I'm believing this will finally happen. Thank you! The last thing we need is a mine when we struggle with poor
air quality and the never-ending concern of a clean water supply. So of course we support the recommendations
of Salt Lake County’s Mountains Planning District and the Salt Lake County Planning Commission to amend the
Forestry and Recreation Zone and Foothills and Canyon Overlay Zones.
It will be great to once and for all prohibit the uses of “mineral extraction and processing; mine; quarry; gravel
pit; including crushers or concrete batching plants used in connection with and as part of an operation for the
removal of sand, gravel and/or rock aggregate in the Forest Recreation Zone and in the Foothills and Canyons
Overlay Zone."
We have many grand children who cannot protect our resources....yet. So we feel obligated to protect against
threats to our water, ecosystems and quality of life. And we are cheering on anyone who is like minded!
