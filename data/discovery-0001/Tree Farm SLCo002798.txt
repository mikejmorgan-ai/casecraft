Below is a statement from the Utah Chapter of Backcountry Hunters and Anglers (BHA) on the
proposed mine in Parley's Canyon. Backcountry Hunters and Anglers is a 501(c)(3) sportsmen's
organization that focuses on issues pertaining to public lands/waters, access & opportunity, fair
chase, and wildlife:
Habitat loss continues to be one of the leading concerns for maintaining healthy elk herds, especially
during the winter months when elk are in their most vulnerable state. The Division of Wildlife
Resources has designated this area of the proposed mine as crucial wintering grounds for elk. Given
the risk of fracturing and disturbing this contiguous habitat which could yield negative impacts on elk
in the Wasatch West unit, BHA argues that further studies on elk population impacts are warranted
before such placement of a mine can be considered.
