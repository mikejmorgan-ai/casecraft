Hello Ms. Carter,
As a resident of Salt Lake City in the St Mary's area, I am writing to express our deep concerns about
the proposed limestone quarry mining operation located within Parleys Canyon. Some of our
concerns are listed below:
Air and dust pollution relating to this operation. Pollution is a serious concern in our area that
is, unfortunately, inadequately prioritized in our state as it is. But, this mining operation will
make a significant negative impact on the air we breathe. The current Kilgore gravel quarry in
Parleys Canyon has a dismal track record of air quality and fugitive dust control, and was
denied expansion by a Utah Supreme Court ruling in 2004
The drinking water in our area comes directly from protected Wasatch Mountain watersheds. It
is incredible that this request is even being considered.
Finally, the fire risk is increased due to mining and trucking operations.
For these reasons, this proposal should be denied.
