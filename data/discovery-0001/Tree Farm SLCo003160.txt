Good morning Mayor Wilson,
First off, let me express my gratitude for your stated opposition to the proposed mine in Parley's
Canyon. I represent a group of (mostly) female outdoor enthusiasts who are absolutely sick over the
mine's possibility. We have been enjoying the wilderness areas of the Wasatch front for decades.
While we realize that some development has been appropriate, we feel that the proposed mine would
permanently damage the landscape, the wildlife, the waterways and the air quality of Salt Lake
County.
We are vehemently opposed to any new mining in Salt Lake County - and particularly so as it relates
to the Wasatch front. We understand that a meeting and a vote will be held next week to determine
the fate of the mining ordinance. I'm writing to let you know that you have a LOT of support in
opposition. We are grateful for - and counting on - your representation concerning this matter.
