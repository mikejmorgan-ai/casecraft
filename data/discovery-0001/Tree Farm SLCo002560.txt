After listening to the intense concerns of thousands of residents about preserving our foothills and canyons, I’m
upholding my duty to constituents by working together with the County Council to align existing ordinances in forestry
and recreation zones with the values and actions we’ve been taking since the beginning of my administration to secure
better canyon management.
This is an ongoing effort to safeguard our most precious areas from unnecessary pollution and widespread impacts to
residents, visitors, wildlife, and the environment so all our canyons and foothills can be safely accessed by millions of
people today and for decades to come.
