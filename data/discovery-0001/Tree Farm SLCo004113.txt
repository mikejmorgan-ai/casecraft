Council Member Granato,
I am hoping to speak with you for a few minutes about the West Bench General Plan that will be presented to the
council for approval in the next few weeks.
I am life long resident of Salt Lake County's west side and have watched as development has crept further west
without seeing any plan to preserve some open space for residents. This newest plan proposes developing over
1,000 acres high up in the canyons of the Oquirrhs. I have watched the county over the years lead efforts to
preserve similar natural areas on the east side from development, mining, and destructive infrastructure like
gondolas. But it does seem there is a double standard and those concerns stop somewhere west of I-15. I think it
is mainly due to a lack of familiarity with the beautiful spaces out here on the west bench.
I would love to be able to share a little more about these concerns and why the west bench plan, in it's current
draft, is not consistent with the vision of county leadership has laid out.
Let me know if we could schedule some time for a short call. If you have any questions or if corresponding by
email is easier just let me know.
