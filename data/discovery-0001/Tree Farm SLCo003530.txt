Re: Stream Alteration Application to Extend an Existing Culvert on Smith Creek, Salt Lake
County.
Stream Alteration Application No. 22-57-05SA.
Mr. Lassley:
Stream Alteration Application No. 22-57-05SA in the name of Tree Farm LLC was received by
this office on February 11, 2022. The application was processed as per normal protocols and
circulation of the application generated a number of comments. Upon review of the application
and comments received, this office is unable to approve the application at this time for the
following reasons:




This office is concerned regarding the sizing of the proposed culvert. Has the culvert
diameter been sized to accommodate current and anticipated flows in the Smith Creek
drainage?
A significant length of channel will be eliminated as a result of culvert installation. What
hydraulic evaluation has been conducted to ensure that increases in water velocity will
not result in excessive degradation downstream?
The aerial photograph showing the proposed culvert extension presented in the
applications shows the culvert outlet at a near 90 degree angle to the alignment of the
Smith Creek channel. What hydraulic analysis has been conducted to show that this
configuration will not result in excessive erosion to the west bank of Smith Creek and the
northbound off ramp to Mt. Aire Road from Interstate 80?

Processing of the application has also revealed that private property ownership coordination may
be necessary to conduct the proposed culvert installation. Furthermore, it is the understanding of
this office that the project will require separate authorization from the U.S. Army Corps of
Engineers and Salt Lake County Flood Control. As a result, it is premature to issue an approval
for the proposed project until the design has incorporated requirements of other regulatory
agencies and affected property owners.
At this time Stream Alteration Application 22-57-05SA will remain in an
unapproved status until the above noted items have been addressed and results
