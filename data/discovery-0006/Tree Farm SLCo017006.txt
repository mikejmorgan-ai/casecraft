Dear Council Members and Mayor Wilson,
As a citizen of Salt Lake City, I have attempted to find something positive about the proposal by Tree Farm that
would create a mining operation in Parley's Canyon. I haven't been able to come up with one positive to be
gained if this is allowed to happen. I am writing in total support of the recommendations of Salt Lake County’s
Mountains Planning District and the Salt Lake County Planning Commission to amend the Forestry and
Recreation Zone and Foothills and Canyon Overlay Zones.
The last thing our city and county needs is an operation that will worsen our air and water quality, add to more
congestion on our highways and harm our flora and fauna. I hope to hear that the Council votes to stop this
operation and others that are similar in scope.
