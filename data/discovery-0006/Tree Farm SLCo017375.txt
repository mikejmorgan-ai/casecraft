RE: Support for Ordinance to Prohibit Mining in Forestry and Recreation Zones
Dear Salt Lake County Council,
Big Cottonwood Community Council supports the proposed amendments to Salt Lake County Code Sections:
19.12.030
19.72.190
19.76.030
This will eliminate mineral extraction and processing as a conditional use activity and prohibit these and other related
uses in the Forestry and Recreation Zones.
The Central Wasatch is an iconic, beautiful, and popular recreational resource for the citizens of Utah. It should not be
subject to private industrial use that has the potential to substantially degrade the forest environment.
Risks from mineral extraction in our canyons include increased air pollution, seismic disturbances, dust, greater wildfire
potential, and loss of wildlife habitat.
We all need to work to preserve the aesthetic and environmental qualities of the canyons in Salt Lake County. Your
support and hard work on behalf of the Wasatch is very much appreciated.
Sincerely,

Barbara Cameron
