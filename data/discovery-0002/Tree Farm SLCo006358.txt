I am extremely opposed to development of the quarry(s) in Parleys Canyon for the following reasons:
1. Exposure to ambient crystalline silica dust produces lung diseases like silicosis and silica
tuberculosis. It also increases the risk of tuberculosis, nonmalignant renal failure and autoimmune
diseases. Silica has been classified as a carcinogen by the international Agency for Research on
Cancer. That quarry would be about one square mile. Imagine the amount of dust that would be
spewed out onto the communities of the Salt Lake Valley.
2. The dust from the existing quarry where I live at 3156 S. 2000 E. in Millcreek already posses an
alarming health risk. The dust produced by the much larger quarry, nearly one square mile, on the
south side of I 80 be gargantuan in comparison.
3. The limestone excavated would be cooked to make cement. This cooking process emits one ton
of CO2 for every ton of limestone cooked. There is not yet a clean CO2 free alternative. Add to that,
the vehicle emissions produced to excavate and transport all quarried material and you have a real
health and environmental disaster in the making. Contemplate this: The last seven years have been
the hottest years ever recorded on our home planet Earth. STOP THIS TREE FARM LLC QUARRY
PROJECT NOW!
