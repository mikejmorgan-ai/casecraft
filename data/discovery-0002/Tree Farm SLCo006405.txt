Dear Ms. Carter,
I want to express my concerns and opposition to the proposed mine in Parley's Canyon. The
tremendous threat to our air quality and health risks are of great concern to me - for myself, my family
and for the generations to come. My family lives in Millcreek, close to the mouth of Parley's Canyon
and we have a cabin by Lamb's Canyon and would surely suffer the ill effects.
In addition, our beautiful mountains - one of the treasures of our city - would be threatened. I am
grateful that the Salt Lake County commission opposed mineral extraction in our forestry areas and
ask that you follow their lead and take the same action. I urge you to reconsider the permit for this
mine, it will do irreparable damage to our air quality, our health and our mountains for now and for the
future.
