Dear Mr. Snelgrove,
Dear SLCo Mayor and Council,
Thank you for taking this important step in protecting the Wasatch Mountains!
I personally enjoy the mountains as they are without extensive mining! And I support transportion systems that
allow me access to nature without a car and minimal impact to the wildlife, land, and water.
Fortunately, between the Mountain Accord and the recently adopted Wasatch Canyons General Plan, there are a
lot of community vetted actions that we need the leadership of Salt Lake County to implement. We need robust
and dynamic policy to protect our watersheds and public lands. We need inclusive and comprehensive recreation
plans. We need integrated transportation solutions that help all users and visitors to our canyons to leave their
cars at home so that our wild and beautiful canyons are not converted into parking lots and transportation
contraptions.
We look forward to working with you to do better in stewarding and protecting the Wasatch Mountains in Salt
Lake County, for the benefit of current and future generations, improving our quality of life and finding that
sweet spot, where man and nature exist in harmony and symbiosis with one another.
