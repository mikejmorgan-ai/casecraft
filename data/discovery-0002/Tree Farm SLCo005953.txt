Hi Marybeth:
Thanks for your interest. It is the involved citizen that makes our country work. Please know that I am firmly opposed to
the quarry up Parleys Canyon and was happy to make the motion to pass the ordinance that will ban it. The air quality,
water quality and aesthetic problems with this proposal are numerous. In addition, there is no shortage of limestone and
places to mine it. Therefore, I believe it is in the best interests of the good people of Salt Lake County to adopt the
ordinance and keep our air and water clean.
Thanks again,
Richard Snelgrove
Salt Lake County Councilman at-Large
