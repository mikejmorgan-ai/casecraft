Request for Agency Action

Pursuant to Utah Administrative Code § R641-105-200, Save Our Canyons, a Utah
501(c)(3) corporation, submits this Motion for Leave to File Petition to Intervene.
As grounds for this Motion, Save Our Canyons states that it did not have notice of Tree
Farm, LLC’s (“Tree Farm”) Request for Agency Action until after the time for a response passed
. Upon learning of Tree Farm’s request, Save Our Canyons reached out to counsel and
determined the necessity of filing a Petition to Intervene, which is filed concurrently herewith.
Save Our Canyons’ members will be impacted by Tree Farm’s proposed operations and
their legal interests will be substantially affected by the proceedings in this matter. It is
imperative that Save Our Canyons have the opportunity to address Tree Farm’s efforts to

circumvent public input with its filing of both a Small Mine LOI and Large Mine LOI.
Moreover, Tree Farm will not be prejudiced by allowing Save Our Canyons to file its Petition to
Intervene, as the hearing on this matter has been postponed until February 23, 2022, and it will
have an opportunity to address the petition.
Because Save Our Canyons has shown good cause for being allowed to file its Petition to
Intervene, it respectfully requests that the Board grant this Motion. A proposed order is
submitted herewith.
