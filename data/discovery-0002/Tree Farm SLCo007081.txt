BEFORE THE BOARD OF OIL, GAS AND MINING

A public meeting will be held on Wednesday, March 23, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. The meeting will be conducted
electronically with an anchor location at the Department of Natural Resources Auditorium, 1594 W. North
Temple, Salt Lake City, Utah, pursuant to Utah Code Ann. § 52-4-207.
The meeting will be streamed live on the Department of Natural Resources YouTube channel. The link for
the Briefing Session is https://youtu.be/QAPaGxByxxk, and the link for the Board Hearing is
https://youtu.be/oQo_q21E_T4.

The Division of Oil, Gas and Mining will conduct a Briefing Session at 9:00 a.m.:
1.

Legislative Update

2.

Rulemaking

3.

Other

4.

Next Month’s Agenda and Division Calendar

5.

Board Working Lunch
Environmental Excellence Award nominations will be discussed and a training session for Board
members may be held, as time allows. The session is open to the public and will be streamed live on
https://youtu.be/oQo_q21E_T4 (The training session was continued from the February 23 Board
Hearing and may be continued to the Board’s regularly scheduled hearing on April 27).

6.

Opportunity for General Public Comment
Members of the public who wish to participate in the meeting electronically or in-person must
contact the Board’s secretary, Ms. Carter, by phone at 801-538-5277, or email at
juliecarter@utah.gov, at least 24 hours prior to the meeting. Attendees will be required to abide by
all public health protocols in place at the time of the meeting.

The Board will hear the following Matters at or around 10:00 a.m.:
1.
Per Acre Bonding Costs for Small Mines and Exploration – The Division will present information
about average costs per acre for reclamation, followed by an opportunity for public comment. The Board
will be asked to approve a figure that will be used for bonding small mines and exploration operations.
2.
Docket No. 2019-014 Cause No. C/007/0045 – In re: Request by the Utah Division of Oil, Gas, and
Mining to forfeit the reclamation surety in order to complete reclamation of the Wellington Dry-Coal
Cleaning Facility, Permit No. C/007/0045, located in Section 14, Township 15 South, Range 10 East,
SLBM, Carbon County, Utah. (This matter has been continued to the regularly scheduled Board hearing
on June 22, 2022.)
3.
Docket No. 2022-002 Cause No. S/035/0053 – In the matter of the Request for Agency Action of
TREE FARM, LLC for a declaratory ruling regarding (1) the completeness of Tree Farm’s NOI-SMO
S/035/0053; and (2) the sufficiency of Tree Farm’s proposed reclamation surety. (This matter was continued
from the February 23, 2022 Board Hearing.)
4.
Docket No. 2022-004 Cause No. 996-01 – In the matter of: Notice of Agency Action by the Division
of Oil, Gas and Mining Seeking an Order Forfeiting the Reclamation Bond Provided by TIDEWATER OIL
