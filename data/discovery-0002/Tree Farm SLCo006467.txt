Electronic Meeting Determination

I, Chris D. Hansen, Chairman of the Utah Board of Oil, Gas and Mining (“Board”), have determined the
January 26, 2022 Board meeting will be held electronically without an anchor location, pursuant to Utah Code
Ann. § 52-4-207(5)(a)(i).
This determination is based on the following facts:
1.
Utah remains in the grips of COVID-19, an airborne virus that has been determined to be a
global pandemic. The Department of Natural Resources Auditorium (“DNR Auditorium”) is in Salt Lake City
and Salt Lake County, which, are considered to be experiencing high levels of COVID-19 transmission.
2.
Based on the emergence of the “omicron” variant of the COVID-19 virus, Utah is currently
experiencing a high number of COVID-19 cases and the Board remains vigilant in following protocols that
control the spread of the virus by minimizing face-to-face interactions.
3.
Conducting a public meeting at this time at the DNR Auditorium with full public participation
presents a substantial risk to the health or safety of those present or who would otherwise be present at the
anchor location.
4.
A vast majority of Division of Oil, Gas and Mining (“Division”) staff and the members of the
Board are teleworking to avoid unnecessary contact with others.
5.
The resources at the DNR Auditorium are insufficient to provide reasonably safe accommodation
for the Board, the Division, and the public.
6.
The Board uses an electronic platform which allows interested parties to view the meeting, hear
discussions, and provide written comment.
7.
Please contact the Board’s Secretary at juliecarter@utah.gov for information pertaining to the
electronic platform and/or electronic participation.
8.
Pursuant to Utah Code Ann. § 52-4-207(6), this determination will expire thirty (30) days after
the date it is issued.
