Please vote in favor of the proposed amendments to the FR Zones ordinance code that will prevent the proposed 600acre quarry in Parleys Canyon. You have received hundreds of emails over the last several months, so I will not go into all
the health and aesthetic reasons for preventing quarrying and mineral extraction in this area.
Instead, below are references from the Wasatch Canyons General Plan 2020, the guiding document for the County’s goals
and reasons for preserving and protecting the canyons, including Parleys.
Wasatch Canyons General Plan 2020
Parleys Canyon:
GOALS:
Ensure the management of watersheds in the Wasatch Canyons to protect water quality and quantity
Ensure that future improvements in and usage of the Wasatch Canyons are managed to protect wildlife, fisheries, and
habitat and to reduce human-wildlife conflicts.
Maintain and increase the conservation of natural lands that protect ecosystems and watersheds and provide
recreational opportunities.
Enhance public awareness, involvement, and stewardship of environmental and recreational values and practices.
Promote bicycling and walking through enhancing the safety and accessibility of trailheads, parking lots, bicycle lanes,
and other public facilities.
Improve the recreation user experience while preparing for future demand.
Support the continuation and viability of canyon businesses while conserving the natural features and recreational
attributes that make the canyons a vital economic impact in Utah.
