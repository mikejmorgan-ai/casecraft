FILED
Remarks before the Oil, Gas and Mining Board
March 22, 2021

MARCH 23 2022
SECRETARY, BOARD OF
OIL, GAS & MINING

Distinguished Board: I am Janet Hemming, Chair of the Yalecrest Neighborhood Council. Tens
of thousands have signed petitions with the hope that your board will be a great barrier – the
successful Maginot Line -- between reason, sanity, common sense on one hand, and money and
personal ambition on the other.
Tree Farm wants to out-maneuver you and use every tactic possible to impose its will. A small
mining operation is merely the nose of the camel in the tent despite their statements this
morning to the contrary. In all the legal rulings recently filed, it is clear you have the authority
to interpret and define your policy-making powers and expand them. The idea that Tree Farm
can reconstitute this beautiful, one-of-a-kind property after destroying it, lacks all credibility.
My request is that you declare a moratorium on processing any new SMO NOIs. And secondly
that you take the legal, legislative and procedural steps to prohibit any and all future mining
proposals in Parley’s Canyon or in similar pristine watershed locations submitted by Tree Farm
or any other company.
For centuries Parley’s Canyon has stood as a shining example of Utah’s natural beauty and
breath-taking scenery. Let’s keep it that way.
Respectfully,
Janet (Jan) Hemming
Yalecrest Neighborhood Council
