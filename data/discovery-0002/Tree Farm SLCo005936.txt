Thanks for your interest. It is the involved citizen that makes our country work. Please know that I am firmly opposed to

Subject: Proposed Mine in Parley's Canyon
Dear Salt Lake County Council Members,
I am urging you to pass an ordinance that would prohibit the creation of any new mines in the Parley’s Canyon area of
Salt Lake County. Thankfully the initial project was struck down, but the smaller mining operation that is now being
proposed is equally detrimental to our foothill ecosystem, air quality, and the water quality of the Salt Lake Valley.
As a long time resident and proud Utahn, I am asking you to consider the negative impacts this project would have on our
community and public health. Please do not let private corporate interests continue to exploit public resources at the
public’s expense.
Salt Lake already struggles with inversions and poor air quality, not to mention the increase in wild fire smoke each
summer. We do not need another project putting particulate matter and pollutants into the air, along with increased
heavy truck traffic in the canyon.
Thank you for consideration and please continue to put public health and interest first.
Best,
