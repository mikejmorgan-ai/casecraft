Ms Carter,
I am writing to let you know my concerns about the proposed mine in Parleys canyon. One of the major draws to our great city
is the mountains. While I see the necessity for mining, allowing a large mine to be developed in the canyon is a threat to this
scarce resource.
The other item that concerns me is the impact to our air quality. In the letter of intent for this mine they state they will not
mitigate dust anytime the wind speed exceeds 25 MPH. Winds frequently exceed this speed coming from the east blowing
directly into our community.
I’m very glad to see the Salt Lake County commission oppose mineral extraction in our forestry areas and plead with you to
take the same action.
Regards,
Rod Brown
